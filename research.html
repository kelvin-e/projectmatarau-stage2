Update

This project focussed on gathering insight and data to support our local community/hapu to achieve a vibrant and successful long-term plan that incorporates our culture, health, education, social and economic wellbeing. To take a look at who we are, where we live, what do we want and when can we imagine that this might happen. The second stage of the project is being undertaken from 1 July 2019 to November 2019 with a gathering of all data from 7 researchers from each community from Wharekahika to Tokomaru Bay.
